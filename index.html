About me

I am currently a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Rutgers University, where I am fortunate to be mentored by Professor Sungjin Ahn. My research focuses on creating general-purpose agents capable of human-like perception and behavior. Specifically, I explore areas such as:

  • Deep Generative Models: such as VAEs and Diffusion Models.
  • Reinforcement Learning: with a focus on Offline-RL and Model-Based RL.
  • Representation Learning: focusing on World Model Learning, Unsupervised Structured Representation, and Visual Reasoning.
